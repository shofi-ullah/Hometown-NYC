Visit New York City

Home to 8.4 million people who speak more than 200 languages, hail from every corner of the globe, and, together, are the heart and soul of the most dynamic city in the world.

Top three activities to do at New York City

Central Park

Designed by Frederick Law Olmsted and Calvert Vaux, Central Park influenced the development of urban parks nationwide and is widely regarded a masterpiece of landscape architecture

Statue Of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a 305-foot (93-metre) statue located on Liberty Island in Upper New York Bay, off the coast of New York City.

Brooklyn Botanical Garden

stroll through all the beautifully maintained gardens. You’ll find different types of plants and horticulture techniques in each one. Enjoy some plant-based cuisine at the Yellow Magnolia Café.
